  • When it is your turn, click on an empty Golf Hole in the grid to play that Hole.

  • The game ends when one player gets three Holes in a row (horizontally, vertically or diagonally.)

  • If you win in 3 plays you get a Birdie, if you win in 4 plays you get a Par, and if you win in 5 plays you get a Bogie!
